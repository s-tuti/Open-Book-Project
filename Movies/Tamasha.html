Tamasha

film by Imtial Ali

Tamasha is about the journey of someone who has lost his edge in trying to follow acceptable conventions of society. The film is based on the central theme of abrasion and loss of self that happens in an attempt to find oneself.

Characters

Ved Sahini

Ranbir Kapoor

Ved is a young man who feels trapped by societal expectations and yearns for a life of creativity and passion. He's like that person who's afraid to show their true self to the world. Throughout the movie, we witness Ved's struggle to break free from the constraints of conformity and embrace his inner desires. His journey of self-discovery takes him on a path of exploration and introspection, as he learns to reconnect with his authentic self and pursue his true passions. Ved's character reminds us that life is too short to live according to others' expectations, and true happiness comes from embracing our uniqueness and following our heart.

Tara Maheshwari

Deepika Padukone

Tara is a spirited and independent woman who believes in living life on her own terms. She's like that friend who's unapologetically herself and encourages others to do the same. Tara's character serves as a catalyst for Ved's transformation, as she challenges him to break free from his self-imposed limitations and embrace the spontaneity and joy of life. Through her relationship with Ved, Tara learns to let go of her own inhibitions and embrace vulnerability, allowing herself to love and be loved authentically. Tara's journey reminds us that true love is about accepting each other's flaws and supporting one another's growth.
