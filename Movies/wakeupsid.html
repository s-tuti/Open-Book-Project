Wake Up Sid

film by Ayan Mukherjee

"Wake Up Sid" is a coming-of-age film centered around Sid, a carefree young man living in Mumbai. After failing his college exams, Sid finds himself at odds with his family's expectations. His life takes a turn when he meets Aisha, an aspiring writer from Kolkata, who helps him discover his passion and ambition. As Sid navigates adulthood and responsibility, he learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and finding his place in the world.

Characters

Sid Mehra

Ranbir Kapoor

Sid is the carefree and laid-back guy who's just coasting through life without a care in the world. He's like that friend who's always up for a good time but struggles with taking responsibility. Throughout the movie, we see Sid's journey of self-discovery as he navigates the challenges of adulthood. He learns valuable lessons about independence, ambition, and the importance of finding his own path in life. Sid's character reminds us that growing up is a journey, and it's okay to stumble along the way as long as we keep moving forward.

Aisha Banerjee

Konkona Sen Sharma

Aisha is the ambitious and hardworking young woman who moves to Mumbai to pursue her dreams. She's like the role model friend who's always focused and driven. Aisha's character represents the contrast to Sid's carefree attitude, as she's determined to make something of herself in the big city. Through her interactions with Sid, she teaches him the value of hard work, perseverance, and taking responsibility for one's own future. Aisha's journey reminds us that success doesn't come easy, but with determination and dedication, anything is possible.
