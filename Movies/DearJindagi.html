Dear Zindagi

film by Gauri Shinde

This is the story of Kaira, a budding cinematographer in search of perfect life. A chance encounter with Jug, an unconventional thinker, helps her gain new perspective about life. She discovers that happiness is all about finding comfort in life's imperfections.

Characters

Kaira

Alia Bhatt

Kaira is a talented cinematographer who struggles with inner turmoil and unresolved emotional issues. She's like that friend who seems to have it all together on the surface but is secretly grappling with doubts and insecurities. Throughout the movie, we witness Kaira's journey of self-discovery as she navigates through life's challenges with courage and vulnerability. She learns to confront her past, embrace her imperfections, and find inner peace. Kaira's character reminds us that it's okay to be vulnerable and that true strength comes from facing our fears and embracing our authentic selves.

Dr. Jehangir "Jug" Khan

Shah Rukh Khan

Jug is a compassionate and insightful therapist who helps Kaira unravel the complexities of her emotions and relationships. He's like the wise mentor who provides guidance and support during life's toughest moments. Through his unconventional therapy sessions and empathetic approach, Jug encourages Kaira to confront her inner demons, challenge her preconceived notions, and embrace life with an open heart. Jug's character reminds us of the power of self-reflection and the importance of seeking help when we need it most.
